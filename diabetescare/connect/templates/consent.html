DiabetiesCare is an app which is used for tracking, it analyzes and collects your diabetes information according to the health data provided by you in the self-assessment. DiabetiesCare will gather data such as weight, height, age, health problems, medicines, and lifestyle through the application user interface.


The purpose of collecting this information through the assessment is to enable the user to create an account on DiabetiesCare by which they can receive customized service based on it.


In what conditions will personal information be shared?

- Personal data is only disclosed when it is required for legal concerns or investigations.
- To safeguard and defend the application's rights or property.
- To investigate or help in the prevention of any law violation.
- We ensure that no personal information submitted will be sold, rented, or transferred to a third party otherwise.
